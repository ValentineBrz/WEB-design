About Us

EnglishForYou is a small social network for learning English.

We firmly believe that anyone can learn languages with EnglishForYou. Our short free lessons are more like a game than a foreign textbook. This is by design, because our main principle is: it is easier to study if you have fun.

Why do you need to learn English?

English is the language of international communication, spoken by over a billion people around the world. You may already have some understanding of English, but we will help you master it better, stay motivated and make sure you feel confident speaking English.
